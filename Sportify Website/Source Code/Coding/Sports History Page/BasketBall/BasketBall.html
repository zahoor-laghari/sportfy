Our History

Basketball is a team sport invented in 1891 by Dr. James Naismith in Springfield, Massachusetts, USA. It is now one of the most popular and widely viewed sports in the world. The game is played between two teams of five players each, aiming to score points by shooting a ball through a hoop.

The game is fast-paced, requiring athleticism, teamwork, and strategic play. Played on a rectangular court with a hoop at each end, the team with the most points at the end of four quarters wins.

Popular Formats of Basketball

NBA

The National Basketball Association (NBA) is the premier professional league in the USA and considered the most competitive league globally.

FIBA

The International Basketball Federation governs international basketball competitions like the FIBA World Cup and Olympics.

3x3 Basketball

A faster-paced format played on a half court with 3 players on each team, popular in street basketball and now an Olympic sport.

Interesting Facts About Basketball

  • Basketball was invented in 1891 by Dr. James Naismith as an indoor sport to keep athletes active during winter.
  • The first basketball game was played with a soccer ball and two peach baskets as goals.
  • Michael Jordan is widely considered the greatest basketball player of all time with six NBA championships.
  • The NBA was founded in 1946 and has grown into the premier basketball league worldwide.
  • Basketball became an Olympic sport in 1936 at the Berlin Games.
