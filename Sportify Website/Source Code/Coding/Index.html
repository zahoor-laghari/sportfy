Popular Sports

Cricket

A bat-and-ball game played between two teams, where players score runs by hitting the ball and running between wickets or hitting boundaries.

Football

The world's most popular sport, where teams compete to score goals by getting the ball into the opposing team's goal using any part of their body except hands and arms.

Badminton

A racquet sport where players use racquets to hit a shuttlecock across a net. It can be played as singles or doubles matches.
